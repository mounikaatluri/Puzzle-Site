Mounika's Proverb Puzzle Page



 



INSTRUCTIONS

  • You have to flip each button in order to reveal the letters. If you want to guess the proverb Click on "Make your guess NOW"

  • To play this game, you can reveal no more than half of the characters of each proverb before make a guess. The more characters you left un-flipped before you make a correct guess, the more scores you will be awarded.

  • After You successfully entered your proverb. Click on "Submit your answer"

  • If you get Message like you are almost there. Then please check the spellings and Vocabulary of the proverb

  • You will have 3 chances to guess the Puzzle

  • If you want to Change the proverb click on NEW GAME at top right of the page
